About me...

I was born and raised in New York. I graduated with a BA in Mathematics from Queens College in 2016.
I spent three semesters teaching at the City University of New York after that. I have taught Elementary Algebra, College Algebra & Trigonometry, Pre-Calculus, and Calculus.
Then I received a BA in Accounting and started working in Finance, as a Financial Data Analyst, at Moody's Corporation.
While working at Moody's, I finished my MA in Mathematics, which is where I was exposed to coding for the first time. I then took a python class online and this is where my love for coding began.
Coding allows me to express myself analytically and creatively while solving real world problems or building cool games. Coding is incredibly rewarding and fun! I look forward to enhancing my skills and building bigger and better apps.

Interests

When I'm not coding, I like to watch European Football. I am a supporter of Manchester United Football Club. I also enjoy traveling, learning about different cultures, taking evening walks with my wife, and looking for the best taco in America.
